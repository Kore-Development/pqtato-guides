    Setting Up¶

    This part of the guide will help you set up your Oculus Quest for sideloading.¶

    To set up sideloading please follow the video guide below. You can stop watching at 5:31. The links from the video are down below and you will also need a compatible USB cable. The SideQuest installation part of the video is slightly outdated though. Now SideQuest just has a simple installer just like any other app. SideQuest also had a complete redesign since the video was made but it should work about the same. If you need help please join the SideQuest Discord below.
